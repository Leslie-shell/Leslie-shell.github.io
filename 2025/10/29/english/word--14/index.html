11.perks| allowance |technically

发表于 2025-10-29 分类于 English ， Vocabulary ， 20220325

perks

noun — special benefits or advantages that come with a job or position

中文释义: 额外津贴；特殊待遇；附加福利。
Examples:

  • One of the perks of working here is free lunch every day.
  • Company cars and health insurance are common employee perks.

allowance

noun — an amount of money regularly given to someone for a specific purpose

中文释义: 津贴；补助；零用钱。
Examples:

  • She gets a monthly allowance from her parents.
  • The company provides a travel allowance for business trips.

noun — a permitted amount or acceptable limit of something

中文释义: 限额；允许量。
Examples:

  • The baggage allowance on this flight is 20 kilograms.
  • There’s a small allowance for human error in the system.

technically

adverb — according to the exact meaning, rules, or facts

中文释义: 严格来说；根据确切定义；从技术上讲。
Examples:

  • Technically, you’re not allowed to park here.
  • The system is technically outdated but still functional.

adverb — relating to the practical skills or knowledge in a particular field

中文释义: 技术上地；在技术方面。
Examples:

  • She’s technically skilled in software engineering.
  • The film was impressive, both artistically and technically.

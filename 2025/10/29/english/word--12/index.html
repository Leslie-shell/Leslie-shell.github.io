11.outcome| slot |assist

发表于 2025-10-29 分类于 English ， Vocabulary ， 20220325

outcome

noun — the result or effect of an action, situation, or event

中文释义: 结果；后果；成果。
Examples:

  • The outcome of the experiment was unexpected.
  • We are waiting to see the final outcome of the election.

slot

noun — a narrow opening, especially for putting something into or through

中文释义: 狭缝；投口；插槽。
Examples:

  • Put the coin into the slot to start the machine.
  • The letter won’t fit through the slot in the door.

noun — a specific time, position, or place in a schedule or system

中文释义: 时间段；职位；位置。
Examples:

  • I managed to get a slot for an interview tomorrow morning.
  • The show was given the 8 p.m. time slot on television.

verb — to put something into a narrow opening or to fit something into a position or schedule

中文释义: 插入；安置；安排。
Examples:

  • He carefully slotted the card into the reader.
  • The meeting was slotted between two other appointments.

assist

verb — to help someone or make it easier for them to do something

中文释义: 帮助；协助。
Examples:

  • The nurse assisted the doctor during the operation.
  • Can you assist me with carrying these boxes?

noun — the act of helping someone or something to succeed

中文释义: 帮助；助攻（体育中常用）。
Examples:

  • She scored with an assist from her teammate.
  • We appreciate your assist in completing the project.

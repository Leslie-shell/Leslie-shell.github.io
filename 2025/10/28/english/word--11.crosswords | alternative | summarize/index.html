11.crosswords | alternative | summarize

发表于 2025-10-28 更新于 2025-10-29 分类于 English ， Vocabulary ， 20220325

crosswords

noun — a puzzle in which words are written horizontally and vertically into a grid, often using clues

中文释义: 填字游戏；纵横填字谜。
Examples:

  • She enjoys doing crosswords in the morning.
  • The newspaper publishes a new crossword every day.

alternative

adjective — available as another possibility or choice

中文释义: 可供选择的；替代的。
Examples:

  • We need to find an alternative route due to road construction.
  • Soy milk is a good alternative to cow’s milk.

noun — a choice between two or more possibilities

中文释义: 替代品；另一种选择。
Examples:

  • You have the alternative of working from home.
  • There are several alternatives to this expensive solution.

summarize

verb — to give a brief statement of the main points

中文释义: 总结；概述。
Examples:

  • Please summarize the main ideas of the article.
  • She summarized the meeting in a few sentences.
